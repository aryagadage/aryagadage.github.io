Arya Gadage

Hello :) I currently work as a Pre Doctoral Research Fellow in the Program for Economic Research (PER) at Columbia University.

My current work is in applied microeconomics and econometrics under the guidance of Dr. Michael Carlos Best and Dr. Haoge Chang.

I hold a BS in Mathematics and a BA in German from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

My research interests include optimization, public econ, finance and decision theory.
