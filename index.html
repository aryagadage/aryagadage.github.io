Arya Gadage

Hello :) I currently work as a Pre Doctoral Research Fellow in the Program for Economic Research (PER) at Columbia University.

My current work is in applied microeconomics and econometrics under the guidance of Dr. Michael Carlos Best and Dr. Haoge Chang.

I hold a BS in Mathematics and a BA in German from the University of Massachusetts Amherst.

My research interests include optimization, decision theory, and financial economics.

During my time at UMass Amherst, I worked as a Research Assistant for the Dynamic Memory Modelling Lab (DMM) in the College of Natural Sciences and the Center for Energy Efficiency Lab (CEERE) in the College of Engineering.

I was part of the iCons program and iSTEP Program as well.
